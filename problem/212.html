P212: Enumeration of all monomials

P212: Enumeration of all monomials
Input:
A polynomial $P$ with $n$ variables, $t$ monomials, and a total degree $D$.
Output:
All monomials of $P$.
Complexity:
The delay between $i$th and $i+1$th outputted monomials is bounded by $O(iDn^2(n+\log(1/\epsilon)))$.
