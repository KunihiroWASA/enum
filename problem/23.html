P23: Enumeration of all minimal feedback vertex sets in a directed graph

Input: A directed graph $G=(V,E)$.
Output: All minimal feedback vertex sets in $G$.
Complexity: $O(|V|^2(|V|+|E|))$ time delay.
Comment:
Reference: [Schwikowski2002]
