P23: Enumeration of all minimal feedback vertex sets in a directed graph

P23: Enumeration of all minimal feedback vertex sets in a directed graph
Input:
A directed graph $G=(V,E)$.
Output:
All minimal feedback vertex sets in $G$.
Complexity:
$O(|V|^2(|V|+|E|))$ time delay.
Comment:
Theorem 1
Reference:
[Schwikowski2002] (Bibtex)
