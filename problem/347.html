P347: Enumeration of all minimal dominating sets in a $P_6$-free chordal graph

Input: A $P_6$-free chordal graph $G = (V, E)$.
Output: All minimal dominating sets in $G$.
Complexity: Linear delay with $O(|V|^2)$ space.

Reference: [Kante2014]
