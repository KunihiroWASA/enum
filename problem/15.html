P15: Enumeration of all spanning trees in a graph

P15: Enumeration of all spanning trees in a graph
Input:
A graph $G=(V,E)$.
Output:
All spanning trees included in $G$.
Complexity:
$O(N+|V|+|E|)$ total time and $O(|V|+|E|)$ space.
Comment:
$N$ = number of spanning trees in $G$.
Reference:
[Shioura1997] (Bibtex)
