P437: Enumeration of all minimal cuts of a given pair in a graph

Input: A graph $G = (V, E)$ and a vertex pair $(s, t)$.
Output: All minimal cuts for $(s, t)$ in $G$.
Complexity: $O(|V|^3 N)$ total time and $O(|V|^3)$ space, where $N$ is the number of soutions.
Comment:
Reference: [Abel1982]
