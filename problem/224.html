P224: Enumeration of all maximal bicliques in a bipartite graph

P224: Enumeration of all maximal bicliques in a bipartite graph
Input:
A bipartite graph $G = (U, V, E)$.
Output:
All maximal bicliques in $G$ in lexicographical order on $U$.
Complexity:
$O((|U| + |V|)^2)$ delay with exponential space.
Comment:
Reference:
[Gely2009] (Bibtex)
