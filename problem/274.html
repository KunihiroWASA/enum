P274: Enumeration of all interval graph of a given graph

P274: Enumeration of all interval graph of a given graph
Input:
A graph $G = (V, E)$.
Output:
All interval graphs of $G$.
Complexity:
$O((|V|+|E|)^2)$ time for each.
