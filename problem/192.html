P192: Enumeration of all $P_3$'s in a graph

Input: A graph $G$.
Output: All of all $P_3$'s in $G$.
Complexity: $O(|E|^{1.5} + p_3(G))$ total time.
Comment: $P_3$ is a induced path of $G$ with three vertices and $p_3(G)$ is the number of $P_3$ in $G$.
Reference: [Hoang2013]
