P133: Enumeration of all basic perfect 2-matchings in a graph

Input: A graph $G = (V, E)$.
Output: All basic perfect 2-matchings in $G$.
Complexity: Incremental polynomial delay.
Comment: A \textit{basic 2-matching} of $G$ is a subset of edges that cover the vertices with vertex-disjoint edges and vertex-disjoint odd cycles.
Reference: [Boros2006] (Bibtex)
