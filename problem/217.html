P217: Enumeration of all biconnected rooted plane graphs

P217: Enumeration of all biconnected rooted plane graphs
Input:
Integers $n$ and $g$.
Output:
All biconnected rooted plane graphs with at most $n$ vertices such that each inner face is of length at most $g$.
Complexity:
$O(1)$ delay with $O(n)$ space.
