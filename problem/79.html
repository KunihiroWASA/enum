P79: Enumeration of $k$ shortest paths in a graph

P79: Enumeration of $k$ shortest paths in a graph
Input:
A graph $G = (V, E)$.
Output:
$k$ shortest paths in $G$.
Complexity:
$O(k|V|c(|V|))$ total time.
Comment:
(?) $c(n)$ is the time complexity to find an optimal solution to a problem with $n$ (0, 1) variables.
Reference:
[Lawler1972] (Bibtex)
