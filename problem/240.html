P240: Enumeration of all minimal dominating sets in a graph

Input: A graph $G = (V, E)$.
Output: All minimal dominating sets in $G$.
Complexity: $O(1.7159^{|V|})$ total time.
Comment:
Reference: [Fomin2008]
