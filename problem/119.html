P119: Enumeration of all bases of a matching matroid in a graph

P119: Enumeration of all bases of a matching matroid in a graph
Input:
A graph $G = (V, E)$.
Output:
All bases of a matching matroid in $G$.
Complexity:
$O(|V| + |E|)$ time per solution.
