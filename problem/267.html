P267: Enumeration of all minimal vertex covers of size at most $k$ in a graph

Input: A graph $G = (V, E)$.
Output: All minimal vertex covers of size at most $k$ in $G$.
Complexity: $O(|E| + k^2 2^k)$ total time.
Comment:
Reference: [Damaschke2006]
