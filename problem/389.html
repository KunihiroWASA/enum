P389: Enumeration of all $t$-ary trees with fixed number leaves in lexicographically

Input: An integer $n$.
Output: All $t$-ary trees with $n$ leaves in lexicographically.
Complexity: $O(t)$ time per binary tree.
Comment:
Reference: [Ruskey1978a]
