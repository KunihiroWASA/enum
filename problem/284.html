P284: Enumeration of all maximum independent sets in a chordal graph

P284: Enumeration of all maximum independent sets in a chordal graph
Input:
A chordal graph $G = (V, E)$.
Output:
All maximum independent sets in $G$.
Complexity:
Constant time per solution on average after $O((|V| + |E|)|V|^2)$ time for preprocessing.
Comment:
Reference:
[Okamoto2005a] (Bibtex)
