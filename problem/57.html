P57: Enumeration of the $k$ best perfect matchings of a graph

P57: Enumeration of the $k$ best perfect matchings of a graph
Input:
A graph $G = (V, E)$ and an integer $k$.
Output:
The $k$ best perfect matchings of $G$ in order.
Complexity:
$O(k|V|^3)$ total time.

Reference:
