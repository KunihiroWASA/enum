P22: Enumeration of all minimal feedback vertex sets in a graph

P22: Enumeration of all minimal feedback vertex sets in a graph
Input:
A graph $G=(V,E)$.
Output:
All minimal feedback vertex sets in $G$.
Complexity:
$O(|V||E|(|V|+|E|))$ time delay.
Comment:
Theorem 2
Reference:
[Schwikowski2002] (Bibtex)
