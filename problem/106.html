P106: Enumeration of all paths in a directed graph

P106: Enumeration of all paths in a directed graph
Input:
A directed graph $G = (V, E)$.
Output:
All paths in $G$.
Complexity:
$O(|E|)$ time per path with $O(|E|)$ space.
