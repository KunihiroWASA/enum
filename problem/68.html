P68: Enumeration of all spanning trees in a directed graph

P68: Enumeration of all spanning trees in a directed graph
Input:
A directed graph $G = (V, E)$.
Output:
All spanning trees in $G$.
Complexity:
$O(|V| + |E| + |E|N)$ total time and $O(|V| + |E|)$ space.
Comment:
$N$ is the number of spanning trees in $G$.
Reference:
[Gabow1978] (Bibtex)
