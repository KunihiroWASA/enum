P48: Enumeration of the $k$ best spanning trees in a planar graph

P48: Enumeration of the $k$ best spanning trees in a planar graph
Input:
A planar graph $G = (V, E)$ and an integer $k$.
Output:
The $k$ best spanning trees of $G$.
Complexity:
$O(n + k^2)$ total time

Reference:
