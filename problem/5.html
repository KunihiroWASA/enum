P5: Enumeration of all cycles in a graph

Input: A graph $G=(V,E)$.
Output: All cycles in $G$.
Complexity: $O(|E| + \sum_{c \in \mathcal{C}(G)}|c|)$ total time.
Comment: $\mathcal{C}(G)$ is the set of all cycles in $G$.
Reference: [Ferreira2012] (Bibtex)
