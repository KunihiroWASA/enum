P85: Enumeration of the $k$ best perfect matchings of a graph

P85: Enumeration of the $k$ best perfect matchings of a graph
Input:
A graph $G = (V, E)$ and an integer $k$.
Output:
The $k$ best perfect matchings of $G$ in decreasing order.
Complexity:
$O(k|V|^3)$ total time with $O(k|V|^2)$ space.
Comment:
Reference:
[Matsui1994] (Bibtex)
