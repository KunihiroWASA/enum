P513: Enumerate all single source orientations of a graph

P513: Enumerate all single source orientations of a graph
Input:
A graph $G = (V, E)$ and a vertex $s \in V$.
Output:
All the orientations of $G$, such that $s$ is the only source.
Complexity:
$O(|E|)$ delay with $O(|E|)$ space.

Reference:
