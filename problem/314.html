P314: Enumeration of all well-formed parenthesis strings of size $n$

Input: An integer $n$.
Output: All well-formed parenthesis strings of size $n$.
Complexity: $O(1)$ delay with $O(n)$ space, or $O(n)$ delay with $O(1)$ space.

Reference: [Walsh1998]
