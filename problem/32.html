P32: Enumeration of all perfect, maximum, and maximal matchings in bipartite graphs

P32: Enumeration of all perfect, maximum, and maximal matchings in bipartite graphs
Input:
A bipartite graph $B=(V, E)$.
Output:
All perfect, maximum, and maximal matching in $B$.
Complexity:
$O(|V|)$ time per matching.
