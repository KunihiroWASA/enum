P436: Enumeration of all minimal cuts in a graph

P436: Enumeration of all minimal cuts in a graph
Input:
A graph $G = (V, E)$.
Output:
All minimal cuts in $G$.
Complexity:
$O(N)$ total time and $O(|V|^2)$ space.
