P282: Enumeration of all independent sets in a chordal graph

P282: Enumeration of all independent sets in a chordal graph
Input:
A chordal graph $G = (V, E)$.
Output:
All independent sets in $G$.
Complexity:
Constant time per solution on average after $O(|V| + |E|)$ time for preprocessing.
