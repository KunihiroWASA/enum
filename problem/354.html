P354: Enumeration of all bitstrings of length $n$ that contains exactly $k$ 1's

P354: Enumeration of all bitstrings of length $n$ that contains exactly $k$ 1's
Input:
An even integer $n$ and odd integer $k$.
Output:
All bitstrings of length $n$ that contains exactly $k$ 1's.
Complexity:
$O(1)$ amortized time per solution.
