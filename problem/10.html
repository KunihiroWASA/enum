P10: Enumeration of all st-paths in a graph

P10: Enumeration of all st-paths in a graph
Input:
A graph $G=(V,E)$ and $s, v \in V$.
Output:
All st-paths in $G$.
Complexity:
$O(|E| + \sum_{\pi \in \mathcal{P}_{st}(G)}|\pi|)$ total time.
Comment:
$\mathcal{P}_{st}(G)$ is the set of all st-paths in $G$.
Reference:
[Ferreira2012] (Bibtex)
