P110: Enumeration of all spanning trees

Input: A graph $G = (V, E)$.
Output: All spanning trees in $G$.
Complexity: $O(N + |V| + |E|)$ total time and $O(|V||E|)$ space.
Comment: $N$ is the number of spanning trees in $G$.
Reference: [Shioura1995]
