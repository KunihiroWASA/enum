P73: Enumeration of all spanning tree in a graph

P73: Enumeration of all spanning tree in a graph
Input:
A graph $G = (V, E)$.
Output:
All spanning tree of $G$.
Complexity:
$O(|V|+|E|+N)$ total time and $O(|V||E|)$ space.
Comment:
$N$ is the number of spanning trees in $G$.
Reference:
[Kapoor1991] (Bibtex)
