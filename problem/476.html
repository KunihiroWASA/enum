P476: Enumeration of all cotrees in a graph

P476: Enumeration of all cotrees in a graph
Input:
A graph $G = (V, E)$.
Output:
All cotrees in $G$.
Complexity:
Comment:
Cotree: a subgraph $S$ of $G$ such that $S = G - T$ for some spanning tree $T$, where $G - T$ is a subgraph of $G$ that does not contain edges in $T$.
