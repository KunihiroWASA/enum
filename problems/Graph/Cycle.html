P6: Enumeration of all cycles in a directed graph
Input:
A directed graph $G=(V,E)$.
Output:
All cycles in $G$.
Complexity:
$O((|V|\cdot |E|)(C+1))$ total time and $O(|V| + |E|)$ space.
Comment:
$C$ is the number of cycles included in $G$.
Reference:
[Tarjan1973] (Bibtex)

A graph $G=(V,E)$.

All cycles in $G$.

$O(|E| + \sum_{c \in \mathcal{C}(G)}|c|)$ total time.
