P324: Enumeration of all connected induced subgraph of a graph
Input: A graph $G = (V, E)$.
Output: All connected induced subgraph of $G$.
Complexity: $O(|V||E|N)$ total time and $O(|V| + |E|)$ space.
Comment: $N$ is the number of solutions.
Reference: [Avis1996]
