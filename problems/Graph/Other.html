P346: Enumeration of all CA-sets of a directed graph
Input:
A directed graph $G = (V, E)$.
Output:
All CA-sets of $G$.
Complexity:
$O(|V|^{2.49+} + \gamma)$.
Comment:
$\gamma$ is the output size. $S \subset V$ is a \textit{CA-set} if, for each $v \in S$, all ancestor of $v$ belongs to $S$.
Reference:
[Kashiwabara1992] (Bibtex)
