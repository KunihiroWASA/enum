P182: Enumeration of all triangles in a graph
Input: A graph $G = (V, E)$.
Output: All triangles in $G$.
Complexity: $O(\alpha(G)|E|)$ total time and linear space.
Comment: $\alpha(G)$ is the minimum number of edge-disjoint spanning forests into which $G$ can be decomposed. If $G$ is planar, then the time complexity becomes $O(|V|)$.
Reference: [Chiba1985]
