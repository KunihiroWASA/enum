Graph / Quadrangle (Bibtex)

P183: Enumeration of all quadrangles in a graph
Input: A connected graph $G = (V, E)$.
Output: All quadrangles in $G$.
Complexity: $O(\alpha(G)|E|)$ total time and $O(|E|)$ space.
Comment: $\alpha(G)$ is the minimum number of edge-disjoint spanning forests into which $G$ can be decomposed.
Reference: [Chiba1985] (Bibtex)
