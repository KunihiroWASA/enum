P501: Enumeration of all $k$-vertex connected induced subgraph.
Input:
A graph $G = (V, E)$ and an integer $k$.
Output:
All $k$-vertex connected induced subgraph in $G$.
Complexity:
$O(k^2\Delta)$ delay with $O(|V|+|E|)$ space, where $\Delta$ is the maximum degree.
Comment:
The overall running time is: $O((e(\Delta - 1))^{k-1} (\Delta + k) n/k)$ time.
Reference:
[Komusiewicz2019] (Bibtex)
