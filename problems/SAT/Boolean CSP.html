SAT / Boolean CSP (Bibtex)

P205: Enumeration of all models of $\phi$ by non-decreasing weight
Input:
A $\Gamma$-formula $\phi$.
Output:
All models of $\phi$ by non-decreasing weight.
Complexity:
If $\Gamma$ is Horn or width-2 affine, there exists a polynomial delay algorithm.
Comment:
Otherwise, such an algorithm does not exist unless $P \neq NP$.
Reference:
[Creignou2011] (Bibtex)
